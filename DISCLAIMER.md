# Disclaimer

This MCP server provides food safety regulatory data for informational purposes only.

## Not Professional Advice

The data served by this tool does not constitute professional food safety, legal, or regulatory advice. Requirements are based on published UK government and Food Standards Agency sources, but individual circumstances vary by local authority area and devolved administration. Always consult your local Environmental Health Officer or Trading Standards for site-specific requirements.

## Devolved Administration Differences

Food safety law differs between England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland. This server tracks these differences (notably raw milk sale legality). Always verify which devolved administration applies to your situation.

## Currency

Food safety regulations change. This tool shows the data available at the time of its last ingestion run. Check `check_data_freshness` for the age of the data.
